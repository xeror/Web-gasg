Joint Venture Partnerships, Employment, Graduate and locum opportunities with Pet Vets - The largest Veterinary Group in Ireland

At Pet Vets and Companion Care we are proud to be the largest Veterinary Group in Ireland with a national network of veterinary practices known for putting pets and clients first. We offer Vets, Vet Nurses and Veterinary professionals the opportunity to take the next step in their careers by opening a modern and well equipped practice through our successful standalone and in-store (within Pets at Home) Joint Venture Partnership models.

You have complete clinical freedom to run your surgery your way whilst we take care of all the support you need to make it a huge business success. This tried and tested Veterinary Partnership provides the freedom and flexibility of running your own practice, but with the business support needed to allow you to get on with what you trained to do - caring for pets. With hundreds of practices across Ireland Pet Vets and Companion Care are also able to provide the very best in employment, graduate jobs and locum opportunities.
